Basic Information

The Visitor Center at Grand Teton National Park is located at 103 Headquarters Loop Moose, WY 83012 and is open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Weather and Conditions:

Grand Teton National Park has long, cold wintersâ€”snow and frost are possible any month. April, May, & June - Mild days and cool nights alternate with rain and snow. July & August - Warm days and cool nights prevail, with afternoon thundershowers common. September, October, & November - Sunny days and cold nights alternate with rain and occasional snowstorms. December to April - Between storms the days are sunny and nights are frigid. Snow blankets the mountains and valley.

Entrance fees:

All passes are valid for 7 days*

Private Vehicle: $35
Motorcycle: $30
Hiker/Biker $20
Entrance is free with America the Beautiful pass.
